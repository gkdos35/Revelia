Helpful Details

If you are reporting a bug, include as much of this as you can:

  • what happened
  • what you expected to happen
  • whether you can reproduce it
  • your device model and operating system version
  • the Somewhere Wild app version, if available
  • a screenshot, if it helps explain the issue and does not include child personal information
