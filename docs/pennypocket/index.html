PennyPocket

Chores and allowance, made simple — pay once, no subscription.

PennyPocket helps families manage recurring chores, one-time chores, allowance balances, rewards, payout requests, and weekly summaries — all on device.

The app does not require a developer account. Family sync uses your family's own iCloud through Apple CloudKit; PennyPocket has no server of its own.
