Helpful Details

If you are reporting a bug, include as much of this as you can:

  • What happened
  • What you expected to happen
  • Whether you can reproduce it
  • The level and seed, if available
  • Your device model, OS version, and Illoria app version
  • A screenshot, if it helps explain the issue

From Inside Illoria
