PennyPocket

Local-first chore and allowance planning for families.

Free for 21 days, then one family purchase keeps the app forever. No monthly or yearly subscription.

PennyPocket helps families manage recurring chores, one-time chores, allowance balances, rewards, payout requests, and weekly summaries on device.

The app does not require a developer account. Family sync uses your family's own iCloud through Apple CloudKit; PennyPocket has no server of its own.
