Common Questions

Does PennyPocket sync between devices?

PennyPocket stores your family's data locally and can sync parent and assigned-child devices through your family's own iCloud using Apple CloudKit. PennyPocket does not run a developer server.

Does PennyPocket require an account?

No. The app does not require a login or PennyPocket account.

Where is my data stored?

PennyPocket stores profiles, boards, completions, rewards, payout requests, reminders, activity history, and local device status on the device.

Does PennyPocket upload data to iCloud?

Yes, when a parent enables Family Sync. PennyPocket syncs parent and assigned-child devices through your family's own iCloud using Apple CloudKit. PennyPocket does not run a developer server.

How do I remove data?

Delete profiles, boards, chores, rewards, one-time chores, and payout requests inside the app. Deleting the app removes PennyPocket's local app data according to iOS behavior.
